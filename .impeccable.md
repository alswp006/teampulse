# TeamPulse — Design & Brand Context

## Design Context

### Users
HR managers and team leads (30–40s) at Korean IT companies with 50+ employees, managing hybrid or remote teams and concerned about declining cohesion. They need quick, low-friction activities that fit into asynchronous workflows across time zones and work schedules. They're already familiar with Toss and seek tools that integrate smoothly into their organizational toolkit.

### Brand Personality
**Effortless, Connective, Playful.** TeamPulse speaks like a trusted colleague—warm and encouraging, never corporate or jargon-heavy. The tone is optimistic and direct: it celebrates small wins, makes participation feel natural (not mandatory), and treats team moments as human-centered rather than HR-metrics-driven. Aligns with Toss's core values of simplicity, clarity, and people-first thinking.

### Aesthetic Direction
TDS (Toss Design System) 준수. @toss/tds-mobile 컴포넌트 전용. 커스텀 미적 방향 없음.

### UX Principles
1. **Minimal friction** — Every interaction completes in ≤5 minutes; no multi-step flows or waiting for full participation.
2. **Async-first** — Design for delayed participation, time-zone tolerance, and catch-up by absent team members; never gate content on real-time attendance.
3. **Transparent engagement** — Show clear participation metrics and team mood signals; celebrate contributors without gamifying negatively.
4. **Psychological safety** — Offer anonymity where appropriate (advice booth, sensitive feedback); let teams build trust incrementally.
5. **Mobile-optimized** — Assume single-thumb interaction during breaks; prioritize readability and touch targets over desktop-style complexity.
